How It Feels

Not an app. Not a gadget.
A ritual you can hold.

Shenpan is designed around the feeling of using it — the weight in your hand, the spin of the compass, the moment of intentional pause before you engage with your day.

1

You pick it up

Weight. Metal or stone. The kind of object that feels like it costs something. You hold it, feel its weight, turn it over in your hand.

2

You spin the compass

The needle or the face spins freely. There's a moment of kinetic satisfaction — similar to a fidget spinner, but with more intention. The physical act of "seeking direction" begins.

3

You tap the NFC

Your phone opens your AI guide. Not a notification — a deliberate ritual moment. The AI greets you: "What direction are you heading today?"

4

You set your direction

You answer. The AI remembers. Throughout your day, it gently checks in — not to nag, but to help you stay oriented. At night, a final reflection: did you move in your chosen direction?
